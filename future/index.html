WELCOME!

Centuries ago there were only a few jobs: people were farmers, bakers, butchers or salesmen. Today there are thousands of different kinds of jobs, and new ones are cons tantly appearing. And the problem of choosing the future profession has always been very important and difficult matter because it determines our future life in many ways. It is one of the most important decisions for every person. .

How to choose а profession that will bring pleasure?

Your future specialty should reflect your interests and aspirations of the soul. It is impossible to enjoy the work, if at its choice you used only dry logic such as "there is a lot of pay" or "my parents do not just like this specialty." You must feel something more, some kind of childish sense of joy, satisfaction, even when you just think about it.
That is why many psychologists recommended choosing a specialty to listen to pleasant emotions after performing certain actions.

"Here is the best advice that can be given to a young person:

Find something that you like to do, and then find someone who will pay you for it."
